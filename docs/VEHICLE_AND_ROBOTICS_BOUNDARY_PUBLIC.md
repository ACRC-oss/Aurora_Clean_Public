# Vehicle-Adjacent And Robotics Boundary

## Vehicle-Adjacent Meaning

Vehicle-adjacent work means observation, context, explanation, and support research. It does not mean car control, driving authority, or autonomous operation.

## Current Boundary

- No car control.
- No autonomy.
- No connection to vehicle systems.
- No actuation or safety-critical command path.

## Robotics Research Path

Any robotics path is simulation-first research. Later stages would require hardware isolation, human supervision, manual override, interlocks, legal review, and separate approval. This repository implements none of those physical capabilities.
